About the Firm

ttadv is a focused law and advisory practice built around delivering clear, commercially grounded legal solutions. We work with founders, investors, and boards navigating complexity in growth, capital events, and governance.

We emphasize precision, speed, and strategic framing. Advice is structured for decision-making, not legal abstraction. We aim to reduce friction and increase leverage in transactions and negotiations.

  • Long-term optionality
